02 / About

A developer who ships.

I turn design concepts into fast, semantic front-ends, then push the medium further with 3D scenes, scroll-driven motion and interactions that make a page feel alive.

I'm a final-year Computer Science & Engineering student at AIUB and a web developer. I've designed and built 64 complete websites for real service businesses in plumbing, roofing, HVAC and automotive, each focused on speed, clarity and conversion, and many powered by a reusable template system I built to standardise the work across 18 niches.
